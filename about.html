About

Our Story

EcoGlow began with a simple belief: that self-care and sustainability should go hand in hand. What started as a small idea in a kitchen — pouring natural wax into recycled jars for friends and family — quickly grew into a passion for creating candles that bring light, comfort, and peace without harming the planet.

Our Process

Each EcoGlow candle is thoughtfully crafted from start to finish. We begin with 100% natural soy wax, which burns cleaner and longer than traditional paraffin. Every fragrance is carefully blended using essential oils and phthalate-free scents inspired by nature — from calming lavender fields to fresh citrus groves.

Once the wax is poured, we let each candle cure to ensure a smooth, even burn. We use cotton wicks that are lead-free and eco-friendly packaging made from recycled and recyclable materials. Every detail is designed to reduce waste and honor the environment, while giving you a candle that feels like a little moment of care.

Our Promise

EcoGlow is more than a candle — it's a commitment. To our customers, we promise high-quality, safe, and sustainable products. To our planet, we promise to always choose practices that minimize our footprint and maximize our positive impact.
